Baby fund FAQ

What does a new baby cost in the UK?

Essentials, pram, cot, car seat, and first-year clothes typically come to £2,000 to £4,000. Mat leave income gaps and nursery costs on top of that vary widely. Setting a target and saving toward it is more useful than chasing an average.

Can I use this during pregnancy?

Yes. Nine months is enough time to build a meaningful fund at a manageable monthly pace. Earlier is better, but starting at any point helps.

Is PacePot free to use?

Yes. PacePot is free and stores your data in your browser. Nothing is shared or synced to a server.
