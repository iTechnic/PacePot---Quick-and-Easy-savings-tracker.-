Wedding savings FAQ

How do I calculate monthly wedding savings?

Subtract current savings from the wedding target, then divide by the number of months left.

Can I track payments already made?

Yes. Add already saved or paid amounts as contributions so your remaining amount stays updated.

Is PacePot free?

Yes. PacePot is free and stores your planner data in your browser.
