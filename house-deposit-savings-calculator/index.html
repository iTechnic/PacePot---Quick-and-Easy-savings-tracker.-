House deposit FAQ

How much should I save for a house deposit?

That depends on the property price and your situation. PacePot helps track the target you choose.

Can I save for fees too?

Yes. Add another target for legal fees, moving costs, or furniture.

Does PacePot store my data online?

No. Your planner data is stored in your browser.
