Calculators FAQ

Are these calculators free?

Yes. All eight are free with no account or sign-up needed.

Do they connect to my bank?

No. PacePot is a manual tracker. Your data stays in your browser and nothing is sent to a server.

Can I track more than one goal at once?

Yes. Open the PacePot app to run multiple savings targets side by side.
