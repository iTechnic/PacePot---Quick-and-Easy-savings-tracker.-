How it works

PacePot takes your holiday target, subtracts what you have already saved, and shows the monthly pace needed to reach your date. You can add saving entries whenever you move money aside.

  • Set one target for the whole trip.
  • Include flights, hotels, spending money, and extras.
  • Use what-if scenarios to compare monthly amounts.

Holiday savings FAQ

How do I calculate how much to save for a holiday?

Take the holiday cost, subtract what you have saved, then divide by the months left.

Can I use PacePot for more than one holiday?

Yes. Add multiple targets and track each trip separately.

Does PacePot connect to my bank?

No. It is a manual tracker and stores data in your browser.
