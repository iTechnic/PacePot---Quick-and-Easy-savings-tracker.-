Debt payoff FAQ

What is the fastest way to pay off debt?

Pay more than the minimum each month. Even an extra £50 can cut months off the timeline. The avalanche method (highest interest first) saves the most money overall.

Can I track more than one debt?

Yes. Add a separate PacePot goal for each debt and work through them one at a time.

Does PacePot include interest calculations?

No. PacePot tracks the principal amount you want to clear. For total interest cost, check your lender's statement or use a dedicated interest calculator.
